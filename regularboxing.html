Regular Boxing

Regular Boxing Classes!

Your go-to class to become a boxing champ!

At Comodus Boxing Club, our Regular Boxing class stands as the cornerstone of our training programs, designed to cater to enthusiasts of all skill levels and aspirations. Whether you're stepping into the ring for the first time or you're an experienced pugilist looking to refine your skills, this class offers a comprehensive boxing experience that emphasizes technique, stamina, and the mental tenacity required of a boxer. Recreational Boxing

Engage in the art of boxing in a stress-free environment. Recreational Boxing is perfect for fitness enthusiasts who wish to learn boxing techniques without the intensity of competition.

Take your boxing to the next level. Competitive Boxing is aimed at those looking to enter the ring, focusing on advanced techniques, strategies, and rigorous training.
