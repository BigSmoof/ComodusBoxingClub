Specials

Special Classes!

They're called special classes for a reason!

Special Classes at our club offer a unique opportunity to diversify your training regimen and delve into specialized areas of physical fitness and combat sports. These classes include an array of distinctive offerings such as private lessons, wrestling, and more, each designed to cater to specific interests and goals. Whether you're looking to refine your technique with one-on-one coaching, challenge yourself in the disciplined art of wrestling, or explore other specialized training options, our Special Classes provide the perfect platform.



Private Classes

At only $50 an hour, private Classes are the epitome of personalized training, offering tailored instruction that focuses on your individual goals, strengths, and areas for improvement. Whether you're a beginner seeking to lay a solid foundation, or an advanced athlete preparing for competition, private lessons provide the undivided attention and customized curriculum you need.

Wrestling Classes

Wrestling Classes introduce participants to the rigorous and rewarding world of wrestling, a discipline that combines strength, strategy, and endurance. These sessions are designed to teach the foundational techniques of wrestling, including takedowns, pins, and escapes, while also emphasizing conditioning, flexibility, and mental toughness.
