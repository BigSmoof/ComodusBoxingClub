Kids Boxing

Kids Boxing Classes!

This is where kids, become spartans!

Kids boxing classes at our club are designed to introduce young champions to the world of boxing in a fun, safe, and engaging environment. Our program focuses on the fundamentals of boxing, including stance, movement, and basic punches, while also emphasizing discipline, respect, and self-confidence. Tailored for children aged 6 to 12, our classes provide a fantastic outlet for energy, help improve coordination and fitness levels, and offer valuable lessons in teamwork and perseverance.



Beginner Kids Boxing

Our Beginner Kids Classes are the perfect starting point for young enthusiasts eager to step into the world of boxing. Designed specifically for novices, these sessions focus on building a strong foundation with basic techniques, footwork, and simple combinations.

Advanced Kids Boxing

The Advanced Kids Classes are tailored for young boxers ready to take their skills to the next level. Building on the fundamentals learned in beginner classes, these sessions introduce more complex techniques, defensive strategies, and conditioning drills aimed at refining agility, speed, and strength.
